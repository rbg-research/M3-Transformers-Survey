## Datasets, Corpora, and Benchmarks

| S.No | Year | Dataset / Corpus Name | Category | Scale / Size | Languages | Short Description                                                                                                                                     |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |:------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **1** | 2020 | **CoVoST 2** | Speech Translation | 2,880 hours | 36 (21 source, 15 target) | Crowd-sourced and built on Common Voice; covers everyday recording conditions and accents for benchmarking multilingual ASR and ST.       |
| **2** | 2019 | **MuST-C** | Speech Translation | ~385 hours/target (3,080+ total) | English $\rightarrow$ 8-14 languages | TED-talk based corpus featuring planned and expressive speech with synchronised audio, transcripts, and translations.                     |
| **3** | 2023 | **FLEURS** | Speech Translation / ASR | ~1,224 hours | 102 languages | An n-way parallel dataset aligned at the sentence level; used as a standard benchmark for zero-shot and few-shot multilingual evaluation. |
| **4** | 2021 | **VoxPopuli** | ASR / Speech Translation | 400K hours total | 23 languages | European Parliament recordings providing a highly formal and domain-specific context for SSL pre-training and supervised learning.        |
| **5** | 2020 | **Europarl-ST** | Speech Translation | Audio-transcript-translation triplets | 6 European languages | Parliamentary speech covering 30-72 translation directions depending on the release version.                                              |
| **6** | 2021 | **Multilingual TEDx** | Speech Translation | Hundreds of hours | 8 source languages | Multi-way parallel sets of conversational, spontaneous non-English source TEDx talks with aligned transcripts and translations.           |
| **7** | 2018 | **How2** | Multimodal ST | 300 hrs audio; 2,000 hrs video | English $\rightarrow$ Portuguese | Instructional videos with speech, English subtitles, and crowdsourced Portuguese translations.                                            |
| **8** | 2020 | **Common Voice** | Speech Recognition | 10s to >1,000 hours/language | 60+ languages | A massively-multilingual crowd-sourced corpus with significant diversity in speakers, accents, and background noise.                      |
| **9** | 2020 | **MLS (Multilingual LibriSpeech)** | Speech Recognition | ~50K hours total | 8 languages | Derived from LibriVox audiobooks; a standard resource for supervised multilingual ASR and TTS research.                                   |
| **10** | 2020 | **LibriLight** | Speech Recognition / SSL | 60K hours (unlabelled) | English | Central audiobook benchmark used for self-supervised pre-training and comparing encoders.                                                 |
| **11** | 2015 | **LibriSpeech** | Speech Recognition | 960 hours (labelled) | English | A foundational supervised ASR benchmarking corpus based on public domain audiobooks.                                                      |
| **12** | 2021 | **GigaSpeech** | Speech Recognition | 10K hrs labelled; 40K hrs total | English | Evolving multi-domain corpus covering audiobooks, podcasts, and YouTube with curated dev/test sets.                                       |
| **13** | 2021 | **SPGISpeech** | Speech Recognition | ~5,000 hours | English | Professionally transcribed financial earnings calls with detailed metadata for formatted end-to-end ASR.                                  |
| **14** | 2025 | **SPGISpeech 2.0** | Speech Recognition | 3,780 hours | English | Transcribed multi-speaker financial audio intended for speaker-tagged transcription research.                                             |
| **15** | N/A | **Indian-language Corpora (OpenSLR)** | Speech Recognition | 20-100 hours/language | Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, Telugu | Features clean multi-speaker recordings and accent-focused benchmarks used to study bias and code-switching.                                 |
| **16** | 2023 | **Svarah** | Speech Recognition | Evaluation Benchmark | Indian English | Benchmark specifically curated for evaluating English ASR Systems on Indian accents.                                                      |
| **17** | 2024 | **LAHAJA** | Speech Recognition | Evaluation Benchmark | Hindi | A robust multi-accent benchmark for evaluating Hindi automatic speech recognition systems.                                                |
| **18** | 2023 | **SeamlessAlign** | Weakly Supervised | ~270K hours aligned | ~100 languages | Automatically aligned speech-translation pairs mined from raw web audio via embedding filtering.                                          |
| **19** | 2023 | **Whisper Training Data** | Weakly Supervised | ~680K hours | 90+ languages | Web-scraped multilingual audio featuring noisy transcripts and language tags spanning multiple domains.                                   |
| **20** | 2019 | **AudioCaps** | Audio Captioning | 46K clips | N/A | Pairs of audio clips equipped with diverse, human-written, high-quality free-text captions.                                               |
| **21** | 2020 | **Clotho** | Audio Captioning | 4.9K - 7K clips | N/A | 15-30 seconds audio samples with five captions per sample, emphasizing caption diversity.                                                 |
| **22** | 2025 | **AudioSetCaps** | Audio Captioning | ~1.9M pairs | N/A | Synthetic audio-caption dataset generated using an automated generation pipeline and Audio LLMs.                                          |
| **23** | 2024 | **ML-SUPERB 2.0** | Benchmarking | 143 languages | 143 languages | Benchmark for multilingual speech models evaluated across constraints, languages, and datasets.                                           |
| **24** | 2025 | **DIVERS-bench** | Benchmarking | Multilingual | Multilingual | Designed to evaluate language identification across domain shifts and code-switching scenarios.                                           |
| **25** | 2025 | **AudioBench** | Benchmarking | Multilingual | Multilingual | A universal benchmark to evaluate the capabilities of Audio Large Language Models.                                                        |
| **26** | 2026 | **HPSU** | Benchmarking | >20K SLU samples | English & Chinese | A benchmark for human-level perception in real-world spoken speech understanding tasks.                                                   |
| **27** | 2025 | **MMAU** | Benchmarking | Multilingual | Multilingual | A massive multi-task benchmark focused on complex audio understanding and reasoning.                                                      |
| **28** | 2020 | **XTREME** | Benchmarking | Multilingual | Multilingual | Massively multilingual multi-task benchmark tailored for evaluating cross-lingual generalisation.                                         |
| **29** | 2018 | **XNLI** | Benchmarking | Multilingual | Multilingual | Dataset built for evaluating cross-lingual sentence representations and natural language inference.                                       |
| **30** | 2024 | **IndiBias** | Benchmarking | Indian Context | Multilingual | A benchmark dataset crafted to measure social biases within language models mapped to the Indian context.                                 |
| **31** | 2026 | **IndRegBias** | Benchmarking | Indian Context | English & Code-Mixed | Dataset dedicated to studying Indian regional biases in English and code-mixed social media comments.                                                 |
